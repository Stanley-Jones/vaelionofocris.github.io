Author

Vaelion of Ocris

Vaelion of Ocris writes allegories where silence speaks and memory endures. His fiction blurs myth, psychology, and the slow collapse of faith. The Unwritten Path and The Crack in the Mask form The Witness Cycle — two meditations on what truth does to us when we write it, and when we keep it.

“This book grew out of countless quiet conversations — a dialogue between languages, shadows, and light — until the story found its own voice.”
