Unwritten Path

This is the most visceral and defining scene of the book. The concept of "chains" is masterfully redefined—not as iron, but as living ink. The crowd’s cheers, which they believe are acts of gratitude, physically tighten the chains and fuel the antagonist's power. A devastating, must-read moment. Witness the scene that redefines the cost of memory.
